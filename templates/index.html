Our Mission..

A vegan lifestyle prevents a tremendous amount of animal slaughter and suffering. It offers a potent way to shrink our environmental footprint, especially in regard to climate change. And a well-planned vegan diet can fuel the highest levels of fitness, while reducing our risk of various chronic diseases. Plus, the food is insanely delicious and it becomes more widely available every year. Finding a large collection of vegan recipes without having to visit many websites can be quite a challenge however, and this is why Veggie Planet was created, to produce a large database of vegan recipes over time with the help of its users. As a visitor, you can use Veggie Planet to browse our entire database of recipes and look for inspiration, but you can also add your own recipes for the world to see and try out!

Why Vegan

Here Are A Few Of Many Reasons To Go Vegan

01

Best Way to Help Animals

Did you know that every vegan saves nearly 200 animals per year? There is simply no easier way to help animals and prevent suffering than by choosing plant-based foods.

02

Healthier Lifestyle

According to the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, vegans are less likely to develop heart disease, cancer, diabetes, and high blood pressure than meat-eaters are.

03

Vegan Food Is Delicious

When you go vegan, you can still eat all your favorite foods, including burgers, “chicken” sandwiches, and ice cream. The only difference? You’ll be ditching the cruelty and cholesterol.
